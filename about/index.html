About me

My name is Lu Ming, a theoretical physicist.

I obtained my bachelor degree at department of physics, Nanjing University from 2008 to 2012. Now I am a third year graduate student at ICQM, Peking University. My research interest is condensed matter theory. Actually I am also interested in many other branches of physics and mathematics, even computer science.

I believe one can become whatever kind of people he wants if he is willing to take time and efforts. Having confidence is also very crucial; don’t be afraid of failure, learn from it.

About this site

I build this site mainly to record my life, maybe I will also write some technical stuff, if I really got something to share. I hope this site can improve my character by enforcing me reflecting on myself occasionally.

Previous I’ve build a similar site using Emacs and Org-mode. I abandon that site because I don’t want to use Org-mode, for it is very complex and not well accepted by the community. Besides, that site is in a mess now, I don’t want to organize it any more.

This site is powered by HEXO and GitHub. The theme I used is designed by HaHack. The logo I use is the Chinese character 鹿 in it’s ancient Oracle form.
